Register

By registering your interest for the Awesome Book Awards 2023 you will be subscribed to our newsletter and receive further information packs relating to the awards and the shortlisted books.

The Contact for each school will be required to distribute the information packs, share the newsletters and updates and collect and submit the total votes once children have finished reading and voting for their favourites.

If you have specific questions that can’t be answered using our FAQs section. Please email: info@awesomebookawards.com
